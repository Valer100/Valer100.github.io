Projects Here are some of my projects:
winaccent
A simple and lightweight Python module for easily retrieving Windows' accent color, including shades, specific window colors such as active/inactive titlebar and window borders and theme. Supports Windows 8.x, 10 and 11 and doesn't require additional dependencies.
Sun Valley Theme Colorizer
A module for changing the accent color and applying some tweaks to @rdbende's Sun Valley ttk theme. Simple and easy to use.
Files & Folders on Taskbar
A simple and easy to use tool for pinning files and folders to your taskbar on Windows 10 and 11.
Volume Labeler
A simple tool for changing the label and the icon of a volume in Windows.
